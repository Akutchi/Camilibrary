Hello there ! My name is Camille (They/She), and welcome to my website !

While I created this website to learn more about the Django web framework, I also used this opportunity to create a website that could showcase every book that I ever read on the subject of gender studies and related fields (at least thoses that are connected to it in my mind). As such, you will find here numerous books whether they'd be more academic or less "rigourous". You'll also find there yuri manga. This is by design as I wanted to list every book I physically have and because they also fall into the "gender studies related" fields I spoke of. However as such, I didn't include every yuri I ever read, a list of which can be found below. Also, I would like to point out that the summaries I do are nothing but snapshots. My thinking is constantly evolving, I'm revisiting books, and so, what I might have said once about a book might be subject to change.

Apart from reading, I'm also interested in computer science, and more specifically programming, and I invite you to go to my github if that's your thing.

Happy reading !
